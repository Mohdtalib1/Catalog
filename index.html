Why

My Catalog.online?

An online platform designed for the Garment & Apparel industry.This solution will Save you Money, reduce your work load and increase your Teams productivity.

Procurement team plays a vital role, since the sales of a company is determined based on the items procured.

Procurement efficiency begins with the procurement team having easy access to all their suppliers in a single window & being able to discuss with the internal team and also with the supplier before purchasing.

Suppliers expired contact details or delay in showcasing their new product to their buyers, eventually ends up with loss of business & goodwill.

With the ongoing pandemic more and-more Retailers are choosing to procure online.
